Sky Data

A Visualisation of Social Media Perception of Weather

The Idea

Social media is a powerful platform to share opinions, ideas, experiences and information. The data collected from these platforms can give us useful insights. For our project we collected data from weather related tweets from users staying in New York (using hashtags). The motive was to draw minor changes from this data on the lowest level which might not be captured by the weather department. Using this data we made weather visualizations for the city of New York
